Greasing the groove — pushups Daily, throughout the day

Distributed pushup protocol

Pavel Tsatsouline's "greasing the groove" approach: instead of one max-effort set, spread small sets across the day. Builds capacity without fatigue. Drop and do 5–10 pushups every couple of hours — between meetings, before coffee, before and after the bathroom. Never strain. Keep it well below failure.

Why pushups matter for running

Upper-body endurance shows up at mile 22+ when the arms get heavy and posture collapses. Strong chest, shoulders, and core hold posture longer. A collapsed thorax restricts breathing — exactly what you don't need at mile 24.
